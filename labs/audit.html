Audit

Audit is the structured examination of a durable decision in light of preserved state. It enables correction without erasure and revision without fragmentation.

Why Audit Exists

Acceleration increases both productivity and error. No system operating at scale remains free from misjudgment or drift.

Audit exists not to assign blame, but to restore clarity.

From Inspection to Understanding

Because promotion requires declared intention and state anchoring, audit is not guesswork. It examines recorded lineage rather than reconstructing assumptions from memory.

Audit asks:

  • Was the original intention clear?
  • Were assumptions justified?
  • Did delegation remain visible?
  • Has meaning drifted since promotion?

These questions strengthen structure rather than undermine authority.

Repair Without Erasure

Correction should not destroy historical record. Repair depends on preserving prior states while introducing justified revision.

When revision overwrites history, continuity weakens. When revision builds upon visible lineage, trust increases.

Restorative Practice

Not all error requires sanction. Some error requires restoration.

When preserved state makes reasoning visible, correction can focus on repair rather than punishment. The goal becomes restoring coherence, responsibility, and shared understanding.

Restorative practice depends on lineage. Without visible history, accountability becomes adversarial. With visible lineage, correction can be constructive.

Architecture determines whether revision fractures trust or strengthens it.

Audit and Freedom

Freedom depends on the ability to revise. Revision depends on visibility.

Opaque systems resist correction. Transparent systems allow disciplined evolution.

Audit as Structural Stability

Audit is not an external control mechanism. It is an internal property of architecture.

When decision lineage, state anchoring, and promotion boundaries are preserved, audit becomes natural rather than adversarial.

Relationship to the Loop

Audit depends on State and follows Promotion. Its purpose is to inform renewed exploration.

Repair returns the system to informed iteration within Labs.
