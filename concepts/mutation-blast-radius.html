Mutation Blast Radius (MBR)

Mutation Blast Radius is the extent to which a proposed semantic or structural change propagates across dependent anchors within the Semantic Surface Area.

The Danger of Deep Dependencies

Even nominal changes (e.g., renaming a core term) may exhibit high MBR when the term functions as a semantic hub. In highly interconnected institutional systems, a simple revision can unintentionally alter the meaning of policies, rules, or governance constraints downstream.

Governing the Radius

High-MBR environments require structured containment. This includes:

  • Pre-mutation reference inventory
  • Criteria articulation
  • Backward compatibility evaluation
  • Authority validation
  • Structured mutation containers
