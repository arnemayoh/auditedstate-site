Inspectable Accumulation (IA)

Inspectable Accumulation is the capacity of a system to increase its semantic surface area while preserving reconstructable reasoning and semantic transparency.

Scaling Without Fragmentation

As systems grow in complexity and duration, they naturally accumulate constraints, definitions, and interdependencies. If this accumulation is not inspectable, the system's memory fragments.

Without Inspectable Accumulation, systems oscillate between drift and dogma. With Inspectable Accumulation, repair replaces rewrite and evolution remains inspectable.
