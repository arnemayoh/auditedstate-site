The Problem

AI systems increasingly produce durable actions without durable reasons. Decisions persist — but justification evaporates.

Shadow AI is intelligence without a trace.

What is failing?

The problem is not intelligence. The problem is accountability under scale. When humans and AI collaborate, it becomes hard to answer a basic question: Who decided this — and why?

Why it matters

Institutions rely on justification: law, medicine, engineering, hiring, finance, and governance. If decision-making becomes faster than memory, trust collapses into debate. The result is not only mistakes — but an inability to repair them without erasing history.

What PKOS responds to

PKOS is a structural response to this gap. It aims to preserve continuity by linking: intention → validation → execution → memory.

This site is intentionally written in common language first. Technical and research material exists, but is introduced gradually.
