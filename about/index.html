About PKOS

PKOS explores infrastructure for accountable reasoning in an age of accelerating human–AI collaboration.

As reasoning processes accelerate, institutions struggle to preserve continuity, accountability, and learning across time. PKOS investigates how structured reasoning records might stabilize knowledge work under these conditions.
