The Architecture

PKOS is structured as a layered constitutional system. Execution cannot occur without validation. Authority cannot emerge without justification. Memory binds every action to its reason.

Soul — Ethics, law, and memory. The governance layer that validates authority.

Mind — Intent, dialogue, and reflection. The human reasoning layer.

Logic — Schemas, tools, and computation. The technical execution layer.

Body — Agents, projects, and assets. The layer that interacts with the external world.

Labs — A protected space for experimentation and creative development.

PKOS is not a logging layer. It is a structural separation of governance, cognition, computation, and execution — designed so that innovation remains possible, while authority becomes accountable.
